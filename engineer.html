ConfyScratchy
Group Discussion Tool for Educators

Confidence Report: For a question with N choices, each student has a budget of N points to distribute among them. Their score is how many points were allotted to the correct answer.

Scratch Card: A small group discusses each question and decides which answer choice to 'scratch' first. If correct, their score is equal to the number of answer choices. Otherwise, the group can scratch another answer for fewer points until the correct answer is found.
