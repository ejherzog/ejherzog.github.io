Strength Assessments

Throughout my career, I've participated in numerous personality and strength assessments along with my colleagues at the request of management. Despite my initial skepticism, I found that these assessments do describe me rather well.

Below is a collection of statements that I feel will give you a sense of my character and professional strengths. Most are quoted from materials provided by the assessment tools, so I have tried to be clear about where I am lifting directly from their work as opposed to using my own words.

“This conscientiousness, this near obsession for doing things right, and your impeccable ethics, combine to create your reputation: utterly dependable.”

Responsibility, Strengths Finder, 2009

“Eva strives to make things better rather than simply accepting them as they are.”

Insights Discovery, 2021

“You aren’t likely to see them continue with an inefficient method for long. It’s very obvious to [them] what is or isn’t going to work, and it can be frustrating when others don’t see solutions as obviously as they do.”

Myers Briggs (ESTJ), 2023

I typically “make an effort to socialize with [my] colleagues and team members” even if it’s just “lunch or coffee together. This [helps me] forge more connected relationships at work, which in turn can facilitate more effective teamwork and cooperation.”

Relator, Strengths Finder, 2021

“She is adept at dealing with crisis situations and will readily take charge, organize, and communicate to others a sense of calm, security, and confidence."

Insights Discovery, 2021

“Eva likes working where she can achieve immediate, visible and tangible results.”

Insights Discovery, 2021
